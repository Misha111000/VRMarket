Meta Quest 2

The Meta Quest 2, launched in 2020, is a powerful standalone VR headset that offers a significant upgrade over its predecessor. It features a high-resolution display with 1832 x 1920 pixels per eye, a Snapdragon XR2 processor, and 6GB of RAM, providing a smooth and immersive VR experience. The headset is lighter and more comfortable, with improved ergonomics and adjustable IPD (interpupillary distance). With its wireless design, extensive library of games, and compatibility with Oculus Link for PC VR gaming, the Meta Quest 2 has become a popular choice for both casual users and VR enthusiasts.
