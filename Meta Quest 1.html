Meta Quest 1

The Meta Quest 1, originally released as Oculus Quest in 2019,is a standalone VR headset that doesn't require a PC or console to operate. It features 6 degrees of freedom tracking, allowing users to move naturally in VR. With its wireless design, it offers an immersive experience through its integrated sensors and dual OLED displays. Despite being succeeded by newer models, it remains a significant step in the evolution of accessible virtual reality.
