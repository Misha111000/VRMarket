About Meta Quest 1

The Meta Quest 1, originally released as Oculus Quest in 2019,is a standalone VR headset that doesn't require a PC or console to operate. It features 6 degrees of freedom tracking, allowing users to move naturally in VR. With its wireless design, it offers an immersive experience through its integrated sensors and dual OLED displays. Despite being succeeded by newer models, it remains a significant step in the evolution of accessible virtual reality.

About Meta Quest 2

The Meta Quest 2, launched in 2020, is a powerful standalone VR headset that offers a significant upgrade over its predecessor. It features a high-resolution display with 1832 x 1920 pixels per eye, a Snapdragon XR2 processor, and 6GB of RAM, providing a smooth and immersive VR experience. The headset is lighter and more comfortable, with improved ergonomics and adjustable IPD (interpupillary distance). With its wireless design, extensive library of games, and compatibility with Oculus Link for PC VR gaming, the Meta Quest 2 has become a popular choice for both casual users and VR enthusiasts.

About Meta Quest 3

The Meta Quest 3, released in 2023, represents the latest in standalone VR technology. It boasts enhanced performance with a new Snapdragon XR2+ processor, 12GB of RAM, and significantly improved graphics with 2064 x 2208 pixels per eye. The headset features a slimmer and more comfortable design, improved hand tracking, and advanced mixed reality capabilities. With a broad array of applications spanning gaming, fitness, and productivity, the Meta Quest 3 continues to push the boundaries of immersive experiences.

About Meta Quest Pro

The Meta Quest Pro, launched in 2022, is a premium VR headset designed for both professional and high-end consumer use. It features cutting-edge technology, including a Snapdragon XR2+ processor, 12GB of RAM, and 1800 x 1920 pixels per eye with advanced optics for a clearer, more vivid display. The headset includes inward-facing sensors for facial expression and eye tracking, enhancing social and collaborative experiences in VR. With a more ergonomic design, enhanced mixed reality capabilities, and a focus on productivity and enterprise applications, the Meta Quest Pro sets a new standard for immersive technology.
